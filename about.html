About Me

Hi, I'm Carrie – an experience designer with over 11 years of expertise in creating accessible, engaging, and impactful learning solutions. I specialize in eLearning, digital design, and fostering inclusive environments through thoughtful UX/UI design. I’ve worked with diverse teams and organizations, from AWS to Dell Technologies, to deliver projects that enhance learning experiences and promote accessibility. My passion lies in designing intuitive interfaces, crafting interactive eLearning content, and ensuring every design is accessible and user-centered.

My approach is driven by collaboration, creativity, and continuous improvement. Whether it's streamlining content development processes, optimizing designs for accessibility, or building immersive learning experiences, I aim to make an impact that empowers users and supports their journey.

Outside of work, I’m committed to promoting diversity and inclusion, volunteering for initiatives like Asians At Amazon (ERG), where I help create branding and multimedia assets that celebrate cultural diversity. I’m always excited to connect with like-minded professionals and explore new opportunities to design meaningful experiences.
