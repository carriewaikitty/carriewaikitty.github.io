Mid-Autumn Festival Email Header Design

A festive email header celebrating the Mid-Autumn Festival with a moonlit night theme and charming design elements.

  • My Role: Designer
  • Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator

For this horizontal banner design, I aimed to bring the beauty and magic of the Mid-Autumn Festival to life. I chose a deep blue background to evoke the calm of a peaceful night sky, with a glowing yellow moon surrounded by clouds and stars. On the left, I added a lantern to bring warmth and festivity, while on the right, two rabbits nestled among flowers look up at the moon, adding a whimsical touch. I used Ortem MN for the title and Ebrima Bold for the event details, ensuring the text was clear while still capturing the celebratory mood.
